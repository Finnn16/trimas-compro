Message From Our President Director

“With over 85 years of experience, Trimas Sarana Garment Industry has become one of the world’s leading manufacturers for apparel brands. Our spirit of quality, care, and commitment leads us to deliver high quality products for our customers, keeping our environment sustainable, and creating a better life for all. Start your fashion journey with us and let our product quality take you higher.” Kartono Budiman (Kevin Oen)

Core VALUES & Purpose

Core Value

Our core values are integrity, innovation, and excellence. Integrity means we are honest and transparent in all our dealings. Innovation drives us to embrace new ideas and technologies. Excellence is our commitment to delivering high-quality products and services. These values guide us in creating a positive impact on our community and the environment.

Purpose

Our purpose is to inspire creativity, foster learning, and promote sustainability. We aim to create products that not only meet the needs of our customers but also contribute to a better world. By focusing on innovation and quality, we strive to make a positive impact on society and the environment, ensuring a brighter future for generations to come.

Evaluation

Our evaluation process is thorough and continuous, ensuring that we consistently meet our high standards of quality and performance. We regularly assess our operations, products, and services to identify areas for improvement and implement necessary changes. This commitment to evaluation helps us maintain our competitive edge and deliver exceptional value to our customers.

Trusted By Leading Brands

Our partnerships with globally recognized brands speak volumes about our dedication to quality and reliability. Over the years, leading names like Jack Nicklaus, Bridgestone, Mizuno, and Srixon have repeatedly chosen us for their projects, a testament to the level of trust and satisfaction we consistently deliver. We understand the importance of maintaining strong, long-term relationships with our clients, and these collaborations reflect our commitment to excellence and innovation.

Since 1992

Established in 1992, our company has amassed decades of experience, making us highly reliable and trustworthy in the industry. Our long-standing presence is a testament to our commitment to quality and excellence.

We use Juki sewing machines, renowned as one of the best and most famous sewing machines in the industry. The results are guaranteed to be satisfactory.
